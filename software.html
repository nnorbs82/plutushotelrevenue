MEWS

Mews PMS has been consistently ranked among the top Property Management Systems (PMS) in the hospitality industry.
In 2024, Mews won the coveted title of Best PMS at the HotelTechAwards, earning a perfect score of 100, which is rare and highly prestigious.
This recognition is based on customer reviews, partner recommendations, and the platform's innovation in streamlining hotel operations.

CLOUDBEDS

Cloudbeds PMS ranks highly compared to other Property Management Systems (PMS), consistently placing within the top 3 on various review platforms and stands out due to its scalability and integration capabilities.
It is particularly popular among independent hotels and smaller properties looking for a cost-effective, cloud-based solution that simplifies daily operations. However, it can become costly as additional modules are added, and some users report challenges with advanced customization and customer service.

SITEMINDER

SiteMinder is consistently ranked as one of the top hotel channel managers globally. In the 2024 HotelTechAwards, it was named the #1 Channel Manager, largely due to its robust integration with over 450 distribution channels and its wide-reaching ecosystem of Property Management Systems (PMS). SiteMinder is particularly well-suited for medium to large hotels and those looking to optimize their distribution strategy across multiple channels in real-time.
