Frequently Asked Questions

What is revenue management?

Revenue management refers to the usage of different tools and processes, such as performance data and analytics, in order to help the hotel industry see how the customer will behave. The data is afterwards utilized in order to make proper business decisions regarding pricing and distribution strategies.

Who is responsible for revenue management?

The revenue management team consists of a revenue manager, the operational team, the sales team, and line-level employees. The revenue manager is the main person, and he/she focuses on generating and analyzing data to make pricing decisions.

What are the benefits of revenue management?

Better ability to predict customer behaviour, wishes and needs, a more effective pricing strategy, along with an expansion of available markets. Another plus is a stronger relationship with other company departments.

What is the importance of revenue management?

Revenue management is a significant concept in the hospitality industry. Why? It allows hotel managers and owners to predict the demand and optimise availability and pricing so that their business can get the best possible financial results.
